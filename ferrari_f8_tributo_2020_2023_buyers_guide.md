## Known Problems & Recalls

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Turbocharger failures | Listen for unusual noises (whining, rattling), check for excessive exhaust smoke (blue/white), and verify consistent boost pressure delivery during acceleration. Inspect for oil leaks around turbocharger seals. Can be very expensive to repair. |
| Engine control system failure | Check for warning lights, rough idling, or difficulty starting. Diagnostic scan is crucial. |
| Brake fluid reservoir cap issue | Inspect brake fluid levels and look for leaks around the reservoir. Test brake pedal feel for sponginess or loss of pressure. |

### NHTSA Recalls
| NHTSA Recall | Campaign | Affected Years | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 21V-833 | 2020-2022 | Brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, leading to partial or total loss of brake function. |
