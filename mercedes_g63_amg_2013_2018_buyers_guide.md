## Pre-Purchase Inspection & Known Issues

Always perform a thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified Mercedes-Benz specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Timing Chain and Tensioner Problems | M157 engine can suffer from timing chain stretching, leading to engine damage if not addressed. Mercedes recommends replacing tensioners and installing non-return check valves. Cost: Not specified|
| Oil and Coolant Leaks | Leaks from the timing case cover are common and can lead to serious engine problems if not addressed. Cost: Not specified|
| Coolant Hose Leaks | M157 engine runs hot, stressing coolant hoses, which can crack or tear, leading to rapid coolant loss and overheating. Cost: Not specified|
| Cylinder Scoring | Reported in colder climates or under heavy load when cold. Cost: Not specified|
| Fuel Pump Issues | Failures of the low-pressure fuel pump can cause poor engine performance or no start. Cost: Not specified|
| Oil Leaks onto Wiring Harness | Leaks from camshaft position sensors or magnets can seep into the wiring harness, causing electrical issues. Cost: Not specified|
| Frequent Consumable Replacements | Spark plugs (every 50,000 miles) and engine mounts (around 80,000 miles) require frequent replacement. Cost: Not specified|
| Transmission Issues | 722.9 7G-Tronic transmission can exhibit harsh shifting, slipping, and unpredictable gear changes. Cost: Not specified|
