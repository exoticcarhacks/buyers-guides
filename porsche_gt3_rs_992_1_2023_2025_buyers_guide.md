## Known Issues and Recalls

Always perform a thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a reputable Porsche specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Excessive oil consumption | Early build (second half of 2021) Porsche 992 GT3 models experienced excessive oil consumption due to a manufacturing defect in the cylinder wall lining, leading to pitting. This could potentially affect early 992.1 GT3 RS models as well due to platform sharing. Cost: Very expensive if out of warranty. |
| Gearbox failure | A 992 GT3 RS experienced gearbox failure twice, requiring a full replacement. The issue occurred after limited track use. Cost: High, requires full gearbox replacement and engine removal. |
| Interior rattles and harmonic buzzing (door cards) | Commonly reported issue across the 992 platform, usually solvable. Cost: Not specified, likely minor if solvable. |
| Battery drain | Consistent issue reported, with the solution being the use of a battery tender. Cost: Not specified, likely minor for a battery tender. |
