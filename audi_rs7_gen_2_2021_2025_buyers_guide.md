## Known Problems & Recalls
Always get a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) from a reputable independent specialist before buying.

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| 48-volt mild hybrid system/alternator failure | This is a very common problem, leading to various electrical issues and potential vehicle immobilization. Cost: $2,000 - $4,000 (estimated, based on complexity of repair and part cost) |
| MMI infotainment system glitches | Intermittent freezing, slow response, or black screen issues. Cost: $500 - $1,500 (estimated, depending on software update or module replacement) |
| Oil separator failure | Can cause a terrible noise and is buried, requiring significant labor to replace. Cost: $1,000 - $2,500 (estimated, based on labor and part cost) |
| Inaccurate fuel count in digital instrument cluster | Software-related issue. Cost: Covered by warranty/software update (estimated) |
| Faulty rear axle alignment | Can lead to premature tire wear and handling issues. Cost: $200 - $500 (estimated, for alignment and potential component replacement) |
| Rear Seat Belt safety retractors | Safety concern. Cost: Covered by warranty/recall (estimated) |
| Dash delamination | Reported for a 2025 model, requiring dash replacement. Cost: $3,000 - $6,000 (estimated, based on parts and extensive labor) |
| Electrical system failure | General electrical issues, potentially related to the 48-volt system. Cost: $500 - $3,000 (estimated, depending on the root cause) |
| Oxygen sensor failure | Requires replacement of O2 sensors. Cost: $300 - $700 per sensor (estimated) |
| Air suspension wear | A known weak point, leading to leaks or compressor failure. Cost: $1,000 - $3,000 per corner (estimated, for air spring or compressor replacement) |
| Timing chain tensioner issues | Can lead to catastrophic engine failure if not addressed. Cost: $3,000 - $8,000 (estimated, for repair or replacement) |
| Variable intake actuators | Failing, causing codes and limiting engine RPM. Cost: $800 - $1,500 (estimated, for part and labor) |
