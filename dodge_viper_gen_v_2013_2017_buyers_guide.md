## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection by a Viper specialist is non-negotiable.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Piston Ring Issues | high oil consumption, engine replacements, solutions include replacing piston rings or full engine rebuild, costs $2,500 to $7,000 |
| Sagging Doors | lightweight doors sag due to heavy power window motors, solutions include adjusting door hinges or replacement, costs vary |
| Wind Noise and Water Leaks | from top of windshield frame, particularly in roadster models, solutions include installing adjustable top latch handles, costs around $250 |
| A/C Airflow Issues | A/C system switches to defrost mode during acceleration due to vacuum leak, solutions include complete A/C system inspection and fixing vacuum leaks |
| Oil Forced into Make Up Air Hose (MUA) | at higher speeds and RPMs, addressed by a recall in 2015, solutions include installing a new oil management system or relocating the MUA hose, costs around $2,400 |
| Water Pump Issues | potential overheating, solutions include replacing the water pump, costs between $380 and $750 |
