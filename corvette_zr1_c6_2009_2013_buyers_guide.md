# Corvette ZR1 C6 Buyer's Guide (2009-2013)

The Corvette ZR1 C6, produced from 2009 to 2013, represents the pinnacle of C6 performance, boasting a supercharged LS9 V8 engine and extensive use of carbon fiber. It was exclusively available with a 6-speed manual transmission. Standard features included Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes. This vehicle offers supercar-level performance at a fraction of the cost of its European counterparts. While not guaranteed, informed buyers may find strong value retention, though the main risk lies in potential deferred maintenance and undisclosed issues related to its high-performance nature.
