# Corvette ZR1 C6 2009-2013 Buyer Guide

The Corvette ZR1 C6, produced from 2009 to 2013, represents the pinnacle of C6 performance, boasting a supercharged LS9 V8 engine and extensive use of carbon fiber. This vehicle is a prime hack candidate due to its supercar-level performance at a fraction of the cost of its European counterparts, offering significant value retention for informed buyers. The main risk lies in potential deferred maintenance and undisclosed issues related to its high-performance nature.

## Pre-Purchase Inspection & Known Issues
A Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified specialist is highly recommended.

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Wobbling harmonic balancers | Can lead to broken oil pump, crankshaft, or even block. Repair cost not specified, but implies significant repair if not caught early |
| Timing chain tensioner problems (LS3 V8s) | Can damage the chain and severely damage the engine. Repair is not easy or cheap |
| Alternator problems | Failure due to age and engine heat, sometimes doesn't charge battery properly. Repair is not a particularly lengthy or difficult repair for a trained mechanic |
| Airbag light problems | Often due to loose wires/electrical connectors under the driver's seat. Simple fix |
| Fuel tank issues (late-model C6, 2007+) | Cracked fuel pumps and/or sending units, sometimes damaging the fuel tank. Repair requires dropping the fuel tank, won't be cheap |
| Magnetic Ride Control problems | Shocks cost around $300-$400 each, plus wiring, sensors, or control modules |
| Roof Panel Noise (TSB 08-08-67-013E) | Snap, pop, creak, or rattle noise from lift-off roof panel (2005-2011 models) |
| Distortion in Vehicle Glass (TSB 00-08-48-005D) | Chemical staining, pitting, corrosion, and/or spotted appearance of chromed aluminum wheels and distortion in vehicle glass (2011 and prior models) |
| Non-GM Engine/Transmission Calibrations (TSB 09-06-04-026B) | Warning against using aftermarket engine and transmission calibrations due to warranty impact (2006-2010 models) |
| Low Clutch Pedal/Difficult Shifting (TSB 09-07-31-001) | Low clutch pedal and difficult shifting issues (2005-2009 Chevrolet Corvette, Corvette Z06) |
| Chrome Valve Caps on Aluminum Stems (TSB March 31, 2009) | Galvanic corrosion from chrome valve caps on aluminum valve stems (2010 and prior models) |
| Chemical Staining/Pitting of Chrome Wheels (TSB 00-03-10-002E) | Chemical staining and pitting of chrome wheels (2010 and prior models) |
