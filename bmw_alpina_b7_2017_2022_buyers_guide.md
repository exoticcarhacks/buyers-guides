## Known Problems & Recalls

Before purchase, a comprehensive Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a BMW/Alpina specialist is highly recommended.

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Turbocharger Coolant Line Leaks | Leading to coolant loss and potential overheating. |
| N63 Engine Concerns | High oil consumption and valve stem seal failures. |
| Air Suspension Components | Failures in air springs or compressors, leading to compromised ride quality. |
| Driveline Shudder | During moderate acceleration, potentially related to fuel quality or driveline components. |
| G11/G12 Front and Rear Air Suspension Struts | Limited warranty extension to 8 years/80,000 miles due to loss of air when stationary, causing vehicle to drop or lean, and acoustic noises (rattling, cracking). |
| N63 Engine Class Action Lawsuit | Excessive oil consumption (primarily for earlier N63 engines, but the Alpina B7 uses a modified version, so general concern is relevant). |
