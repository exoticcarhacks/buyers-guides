# Ferrari 360 Challenge Stradale 2003-2004 Buyer Guide

The Ferrari 360 Challenge Stradale is a track-focused, limited-edition variant of the 360 Modena, designed for enthusiasts seeking a more visceral driving experience. It\"s a prime hack candidate due to its rarity, performance pedigree, and the potential for appreciation in the collector market. The main risk lies in the specialized maintenance requirements and the potential for high repair costs associated with its bespoke components.
