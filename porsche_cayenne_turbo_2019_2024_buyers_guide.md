# Porsche Cayenne Turbo 2019-2024 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Porsche Cayenne Turbo, spanning model years 2019-2024, represents a compelling blend of high-performance SUV dynamics and luxury, making it a strong candidate for the 'Exotic Car Hack' philosophy. This vehicle offers supercar-level acceleration and handling in a practical, family-friendly package, often depreciating significantly in its early years, thus presenting an opportunity for savvy buyers. The main risk associated with this model is the potential for high maintenance and repair costs, particularly for complex powertrain and electronic systems, which necessitates thorough pre-purchase inspection and careful option selection.

## Known Problems and Recalls
It is imperative to conduct a thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a reputable Porsche specialist before any purchase.

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|

| Jerky transmission lurching | Check transmission fluid levels, service history, and potential software updates or mechanical issues. |
| Bose system rattling | Inspect speakers and interior panels for loose components. |
| Battery replacement | Inquire about recent battery service; check for proper retrofit if applicable. |
| Condensador replacement | Common for 2019-2022 models; check AC system performance and service records. |

| Steering column creaking | Check for worn components or lubrication issues in the steering column. |
