# Bentley Bentayga 2017-2025 Buyer Guide

## Known Problems and Recalls

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a Bentley specialist is non-negotiable.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check | Estimated Cost (USD) |
|-------|--------------------|----------------------|
| **W12 Engine Turbochargers** | For 2017-2019 W12 models, turbocharger failure has been reported. Inspect for excessive smoke, loss of power, or unusual noises. | $18,750 [1] |
| **Steering Rack Failure** | For 2017-2019 W12 models, steering rack issues have been noted. Check for heavy steering, clunking noises, or power steering fluid leaks. | $11,250 [1] |
| **Coolant System Leaks** | For 2017-2019 W12 models, coolant system leaks are a known issue. Look for coolant puddles, overheating, or low coolant levels. | $5,000 [1] |
| **Air Suspension System** | Common issues include air leaks in suspension bags, valve block malfunctions, or compressor failure. Symptoms include uneven ride height, vehicle sitting too low, noisy compressor, or warning lights. Repair costs can range from $4,000 to $6,000 per corner. | $4,000 - $6,000 per corner [6, 7] |
| **Electrical and Infotainment System Failures** | This can manifest as touchscreen malfunctions, Bluetooth connectivity issues, random system reboots, parking sensor failures, reverse camera issues, or adaptive cruise control malfunctions. An ABS controller repair was reported at $8,900. | Varies, e.g., $8,900 for ABS controller [13] |
| **Cooling System Leaks (General)** | Beyond W12 specific issues, general cooling system leaks can occur. One report indicates a 100% failure rate for a Bentley cooling system, costing around $4,000 to replace. | $4,000 [14] |
