# Mercedes AMG GT R 2018-2020 Buyer Guide

The Mercedes AMG GT R 2018-2020 is a track-focused machine that brings supercar performance to a more attainable price point. It stands as a prime candidate for car hacking due to its depreciation curve and the sheer performance it offers for the money. The main risk lies in the potential for high maintenance costs and complex issues typical of high-performance AMG vehicles.

## Conclusion

The Mercedes AMG GT R is a **strong hack candidate**. Its main risk is the potential for expensive repairs on its highly complex powertrain and electronics. The best ownership logic involves acquiring a well-optioned example with a documented service history, ideally one that has already taken its initial depreciation hit.
