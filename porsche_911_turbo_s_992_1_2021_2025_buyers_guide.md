## Known Problems & Recalls

**Perform a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified Porsche specialist.**

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| PDK shift fork distance sensor failure | Internal sensor housing deforms from heat cycling, causing limp mode and costing $3,850 - $10,500 |
| PCCB ceramic brake disc replacement | Ceramic discs wear out and cost approximately $6,380 - $11,660 each at dealer |
| Center-lock wheel recall (Sep-Dec 2023 production) | Stop-drive recall on center-lock equipped 992s built late 2023, costing $0 - $583 |
| Keyless entry / PADM electronics quirks | Door locks and engine mount control module problems, costing $350 - $2,100 |
| Audio system speaker failures | Speaker, insulation, or wiring failures in Burmester/BOSE systems, costing $466 - $1,750 |
| Injector failures and misfiring | TSB exists for injector failure causing misfire under load, costing $1,750 - $4,660 |
