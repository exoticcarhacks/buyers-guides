# McLaren 600LT 2019-2021 Buyer Guide

The McLaren 600LT, a track-focused derivative of the already potent 570S, represents a compelling "Longtail" variant designed for enthusiasts seeking raw performance and exclusivity. As a hack candidate, its limited production and strong performance credentials offer potential for value retention, but its highly specialized nature and the inherent complexities of McLaren ownership introduce significant risks, particularly concerning maintenance and potential for rapid depreciation if not carefully managed.

## Conclusion

The McLaren 600LT is a **strong hack candidate** for the discerning enthusiast who understands its niche. Its main risk lies in the high cost of specialized maintenance and potential for rapid depreciation if not acquired and managed strategically. The best ownership logic involves buying a well-optioned example with a documented service history, ideally with the Vehicle Lift option, and being prepared for the realities of supercar ownership.
