# Ford Shelby GT500 2020-2022 Buyer Guide

The Ford Shelby GT500, a track-focused beast powered by a supercharged 5.2L V8, represents the pinnacle of Mustang performance. As a hack candidate, its raw power and limited production make it appealing, but the complexity of its advanced powertrain and potential for hard use introduce a significant risk of costly mechanical issues if not thoroughly vetted.

## Conclusion

The Ford Shelby GT500 is a **strong hack candidate** for those seeking extreme performance with a clear exit strategy. The main risk lies in the potential for significant mechanical issues due to its high-strung nature and the likelihood of hard use by previous owners. Best ownership logic involves acquiring a well-optioned example, ideally with the Carbon Fiber Track Pack, and maintaining a meticulous service history to mitigate risks and maximize resale value.
