## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues

Always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist familiar with high-performance Audi vehicles.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Brake clicking noise | Check for TSB #2047140/3. Listen for clicking from brake pads, especially during light braking or direction changes. |
| Control arm premature wear | Inspect for steering wheel judder or unusual noises during steering. Check for excessive play in control arm bushings. |
| Mag ride suspension leak (non-Plus models) | While the GT has a specific suspension setup, inspect for any signs of fluid leaks around the dampers or worn bushings, particularly if aftermarket coil-overs are not present. |
| Paddle shift selector faulty | Test transmission response during aggressive downshifting. Ensure the selected gear holds without hesitation or unexpected shifts. |
