# McLaren 765LT 2021-2022 Buyer Guide

## 1. Introduction
The McLaren 765LT, produced from 2021 to 2022, represents the pinnacle of McLaren's 'Longtail' philosophy, offering an intensified driving experience over the already potent 720S. As a limited-production, track-focused supercar, it presents a unique opportunity for the discerning enthusiast seeking extreme performance and potential appreciation, though its specialized nature and high-performance components introduce specific ownership considerations and risks.
