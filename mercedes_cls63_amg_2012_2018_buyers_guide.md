# Mercedes CLS63 AMG 2012-2018 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Mercedes CLS63 AMG 2012-2018 is a high-performance luxury sedan that blends executive comfort with raw AMG power. It stands out as a hack candidate due to its significant depreciation from new, offering a compelling performance-per-dollar ratio in the used market. The main risk associated with this model is the potential for expensive engine-related issues, particularly with the M157 engine.

## Conclusion
The Mercedes CLS63 AMG 2012-2018 is a **strong hack candidate**. Its main risk lies in the potential for high-cost engine repairs, particularly related to the M157 engine's known issues. Best ownership logic involves diligent pre-purchase inspection, proactive maintenance, and budgeting for potential repairs to fully leverage its depreciated value and enjoy its exceptional performance.

## Car Hacking Shortcuts
* Years to buy: 2014-2018 (for S model and facelift improvements)
* Options to look for: AMG Performance Package (2012-2013), 4MATIC AWD (from 2014), Driver Assistance Package
* PPI focus: Thorough inspection of the M157 engine for oil leaks, timing chain issues, and cylinder scoring. Check for proper functioning of all electronics and infotainment.
* Known recalls: Verify all NHTSA recalls (including 15V-137, 15V-088, 15V-505, 16V-740, 17V-078) have been addressed via VIN.
* Market forecast: Stable to slight depreciation, strong value retention for well-maintained examples with desirable options.
