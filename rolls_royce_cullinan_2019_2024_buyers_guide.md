# Rolls-Royce Cullinan 2019-2024 Buyer Guide

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan, introduced in 2019, represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury SUVs, offering unparalleled comfort and a commanding presence. Despite its lofty price tag, its depreciation curve can present a unique opportunity for the discerning car hacker, making it a candidate for strategic acquisition. The main risk associated with the Cullinan lies in its highly specialized and expensive maintenance, particularly concerning its complex air suspension system and advanced electronics.
