## Standard Features (2019-2024)

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan comes exceptionally well-equipped from the factory, reflecting its ultra-luxury positioning. While customization options are vast, key standard features across the model years typically include:

*   **Powertrain:** 6.75L Twin-Turbo V12 engine, 8-speed automatic transmission, all-wheel drive.
*   **Comfort & Convenience:** Adaptive air suspension, panoramic glass roof (often standard or a highly popular option), power-closing coach doors, heated and ventilated seats, multi-zone climate control, premium audio system, navigation, infotainment system with rotary controller.
*   **Safety & Driver Assistance:** A suite of driver assistance features such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, night vision, and a 360-degree camera system. (Specific features may vary by year and region).
*   **Interior:** High-quality leather upholstery, extensive wood or carbon fiber trim, lambswool floor mats, power-adjustable rear seats.
