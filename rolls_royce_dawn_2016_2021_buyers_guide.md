# Rolls-Royce Dawn 2016-2021 Buyer Guide

The Rolls-Royce Dawn, a luxurious convertible, embodies opulence and effortless motoring. It stands as a hack candidate due to its significant depreciation from new, offering a compelling entry point into ultra-luxury convertible ownership. The main risk lies in the potential for high maintenance and repair costs associated with its complex engineering and bespoke components.
