# Rolls-Royce Dawn 2016-2021 Buyer Guide

The Rolls-Royce Dawn, a luxurious convertible, embodies opulence and effortless motoring. Its significant depreciation from new can offer a compelling entry point into ultra-luxury convertible ownership for discerning buyers. The main risk lies in the potential for high maintenance and repair costs associated with its complex engineering and bespoke components. Note that 2021 was the final model year for the Dawn in North America.

## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a Rolls-Royce specialist is crucial before any purchase.

## Conclusion

The Rolls-Royce Dawn presents an interesting proposition for buyers seeking ultra-luxury at a more accessible price point due to its depreciation. Its main risk is the potential for high running costs, though careful selection can mitigate this. Best ownership logic involves acquiring a well-optioned example that has already taken its initial depreciation hit, focusing on condition and maintenance history.

## Car Hacking Shortcuts

* Years to buy: 2016-2021 (focus on post-depreciation models)
* Options to look for: Starlight Headliner, Advanced Driver Assistance, Bespoke Audio, Spirit of Ecstasy Customization
* Preferred colors: Verify by window sticker (color preferences are subjective, but certain bespoke colors may appeal to a broader market)
* PPI focus: Engine, transmission, convertible roof mechanism, electronics, suspension, and battery health.
* Known recalls: 17V115000, 16V914000 (verify by VIN)
