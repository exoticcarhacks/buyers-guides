# Ferrari 812 Superfast and GTS 2018-2024 Buyer Guide

## 1. Introduction

The Ferrari 812 Superfast and GTS (2018-2024) represent the pinnacle of Ferrari's front-engine V12 grand tourers, offering a blend of blistering performance and exotic luxury. As the last naturally aspirated V12 production cars from Ferrari, they stand as significant hack candidates for those seeking a modern classic with immense power and a visceral driving experience. The main risk lies in the complexity of their advanced electronics and the potential for high maintenance costs if not properly vetted.
