# Nissan GT-R 2009-2024 Buyer Guide
The Nissan GT-R, affectionately known as 'Godzilla,' is a supercar slayer that has consistently delivered performance far exceeding its price point, making it an excellent hack candidate. Its advanced all-wheel-drive system and twin-turbo V6 offer exhilarating speed and handling, but the main risk lies in the complexity and potential cost of maintaining its highly specialized powertrain, particularly the dual-clutch transmission.

## Conclusion
The Nissan GT-R remains a **strong hack candidate** for those seeking supercar performance without the exorbitant price tag. The main risk is the potential for high maintenance costs, particularly with the transmission. Best ownership logic involves finding a well-maintained example with a documented service history, ideally from a knowledgeable enthusiast, and budgeting for potential powertrain services.
