# Lamborghini LP570 Superleggera and Performante 2011-2014 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Lamborghini LP570 Superleggera and Performante, produced from 2011 to 2014, represent the pinnacle of the Gallardo platform's lightweight, track-focused evolution. These models are prime hack candidates due to their limited production, exotic appeal, and the potential for significant value retention, offering a visceral driving experience unmatched by many contemporaries. The main risk lies in the specialized maintenance requirements and the potential for costly repairs, particularly with the E-gear transmission.

## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues

Always get a comprehensive PPI from a qualified Lamborghini specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|:------|:-------------------| 
| Transmission issues | E-gear transmission can have slipping gears, difficulty shifting, worn components, or TCM problems. Premature clutch wear if not driven like a manual. Check for smooth shifts and any warning lights. |
| Brake pad squealing | Requires cleaning, noise-reduction lubricant, and deglazing rotors. Not a mechanical failure, but an annoyance. |
| Oil leaks | Particularly around the oil pan and rear main seal in early models. Inspect the underside of the engine for any signs of leaks. |
| Paintwork issue | Potential for glaring mismatch, check door jams and hinges for shoddy repairs. Look for consistent paint finish across all panels. |
| Wheels and tires | Highly sensitive to tire pressure differences, aftermarket wheels can cause issues with AWD system, leading to premature clutch and differential wear. Ensure correct OEM wheels and proper tire pressure. |
| Aftermarket sound system | Factory radios not great, improper wiring can lead to electrical draws. Check for proper functionality of all electronics. |
| Front-end scrape | Low ground clearance, front-end lift system introduced in 2005. Inspect underside of front bumper for scrapes and cracks, especially if the car lacks the lift system. |
| Mirrors | Windshield-mounted rearview mirrors susceptible to leaks, auto-dimming fluid degradation. Check for any fluid discoloration or leaks around the mirror. |
