## Introduction
The Porsche 911 Turbo S 991.2, produced from 2017 to 2019, represents the pinnacle of Porsche's 911 performance, offering blistering acceleration and track-ready dynamics in a surprisingly usable package. ## Known Problems & Recalls

Always perform a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified Porsche specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Oil feed issues leading to turbo failure | This is a significant concern, especially for cars that are sparingly used. The replacement turbos are the same part, meaning the issue can recur. Specialists have reported a high incidence of turbo failures. A comprehensive warranty is highly recommended, and ensure the PPI specifically checks for any signs or history of turbo-related issues. |
| Fragile oil pumps | Often associated with the turbocharger issues, oil pump failures can lead to catastrophic engine damage. Ensure the PPI includes a thorough inspection of the oil system's health. |
