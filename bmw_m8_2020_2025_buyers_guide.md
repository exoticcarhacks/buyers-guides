## 8. Conclusion
The BMW M8 is a **strong hack candidate** for those seeking immense performance and luxury at a depreciated price point. The main risk is the potential for significant repair costs, particularly related to specialized M components. The best ownership logic involves diligent pre-purchase inspection, setting aside a maintenance fund, and understanding that while depreciation is your friend, running costs can be substantial.
