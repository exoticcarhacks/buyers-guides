## Known Problems and NHTSA Recalls

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| AC Compressor failure | Common BMW V8 issue; check for proper AC function and unusual noises from the compressor. |
| Water pump/pulley seized | Check service records for replacement; listen for unusual noises from the engine bay, especially a grinding or squealing sound. |
| M sport diff failed | Listen for clunking or grinding noises from the rear differential, particularly during turns or acceleration. Check for proper differential operation. |
| AC vent broke | Inspect all AC vents for proper function of louvers and physical integrity. |
| Rear center armrest broke | Check the rear armrest for functionality and damage to hinges or latches. |
| Batteries died | Check battery health, especially on older models or those with infrequent use. Look for slow cranking or electrical glitches. |
| Oil pan leak | Inspect for oil leaks around the oil pan gasket, often visible as drips or residue on the underside of the engine. |
| Rear suspension issues | Listen for creaks, groans, or unusual handling characteristics over bumps. Inspect bushings and air springs (if equipped) for wear or leaks. |
| Front Control Arms rubber seal damage | Inspect control arm bushings for cracks, tears, or excessive wear, which can lead to vague steering or clunking noises. |
| Engine and turbos failure | Any signs of reduced power, excessive blue or white smoke from the exhaust, or unusual engine noises (e.g., knocking, whining) warrant immediate investigation. |
| Electrical component noise and drivetrain light | Check for any persistent warning lights (e.g., Check Engine, Drivetrain Malfunction) or intermittent electrical malfunctions. Scan for fault codes. |
| Suspension related errors | Check for warning lights related to suspension (e.g., adaptive suspension malfunction) or uneven ride height. |
| Differential oil leak | Inspect the differential housing and seals for any signs of oil leakage. |
| Oil Leaks & Gasket Failures | Common in BMW V8s; inspect valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, and turbo oil lines for leaks, which can manifest as burning oil smell or visible drips. |
| Transmission & Gearbox Issues | Check for smooth shifts without hesitation, harshness, or clunking. Test for shuddering during acceleration or delayed engagement. |
| Cooling System Failures | Monitor coolant levels and engine temperature. Inspect the water pump, thermostat housing, and coolant lines (especially near turbos) for leaks or cracks. |
| Electrical System Problems & Fault Codes | Scan for specific fault codes using a diagnostic tool. Common gremlins can include issues with infotainment, sensors, or lighting modules. |
| Transfer Case Problems | Check for smooth power delivery, without jerking or binding during turns, especially at low speeds. Listen for grinding or clunking noises. |
| Sunroof & Drainage Issues | Check sunroof operation for smooth movement. Inspect drainage channels for blockages, which can lead to water ingress into the cabin. |
