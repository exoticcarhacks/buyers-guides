## Known Problems and NHTSA Recalls

Always get a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) from a reputable independent BMW specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| AC Compressor failure | Common BMW V8 issue, check for proper AC function and unusual noises. |
| Water pump/pulley seized | Check service records for replacement, listen for unusual noises from the engine bay. |
| M sport diff failed | Listen for clunking or grinding noises from the rear, check for proper differential operation. |
| AC vent broke | Inspect all AC vents for proper function and physical integrity. |
| Rear center armrest broke | Check the rear armrest for functionality and damage. |
| Batteries died | Check battery health, especially on older models or those with infrequent use. |
| Oil pan leak | Inspect for oil leaks around the oil pan. |
| Rear suspension issues | Listen for creaks, groans, or unusual handling characteristics. |
| Preventative oil temp regulator / rod bearings change out | Inquire about preventative maintenance, especially for higher mileage vehicles. |
| Front Control Arms rubber seal damage | Inspect control arm bushings for cracks or wear. |
| Engine and turbos failure | Any signs of reduced power, excessive smoke, or unusual engine noises warrant immediate investigation. |
| Electrical component noise and drivetrain light | Check for any persistent warning lights or electrical malfunctions. |
| Suspension related errors | Check for warning lights related to suspension or uneven ride height. |
| Diff oil leak | Inspect the differential for any signs of oil leakage. |
| Oil Leaks & Gasket Failures | Common in BMW V8s, inspect all gaskets for leaks. |
| Transmission & Gearbox Issues | Check for smooth shifts, no hesitation or clunking. |
| Cooling System Failures | Monitor coolant levels and engine temperature, check for leaks. |
| Electrical System Problems & Fault Codes | Scan for fault codes, check all electrical components. |
| Transfer Case Problems | Check for smooth power delivery, no jerking or binding during turns. |
| Sunroof & Drainage Issues | Check sunroof operation and for any signs of water ingress. |
