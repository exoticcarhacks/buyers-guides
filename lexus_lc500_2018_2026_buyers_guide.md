## Known Problems and Recalls

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Fuel Pump Failure | May stop operating, leading to vehicle stall. Check if recall 20V-012 or 20V-682 has been performed. |
| Backup Camera Condensation | Intermittent condensation buildup inside the lens. Check camera functionality and clarity. |
| HVAC Servo Motor Malfunction | Requires cleaning, regreasing, or replacement of servo motors. Listen for unusual noises from the HVAC system or check for inconsistent temperature control. |
| Roof Body Creak | Requires adding shims or C-Pillar work. Listen for creaking noises from the roof area, especially over uneven surfaces. |
| Popping Noises from B Pillar | Requires removing back seat, adding welds, and greasing bolts. Listen for popping sounds from the B-pillar area. |
| Bolts coming off the Rear Spoiler | Requires re-securing or replacing bolts. Visually inspect the rear spoiler for looseness or missing bolts. |
| Door Handle Actuator Failure | Actuator that pops in and out fails. Test door handle operation multiple times. |
| Transmission Clunkiness | At lower speeds and normal driving. Pay close attention to transmission shifts during a test drive. |
| Squeaky Performance Brake Pads | When pulling out. Listen for brake squeal during low-speed maneuvers. |
