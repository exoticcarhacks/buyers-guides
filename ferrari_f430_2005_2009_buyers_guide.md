## Known Problems and Recalls

Always get a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) from a Ferrari specialist.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Exhaust Manifold Failures | Cracks and exhaust leaks are common, leading to potential engine damage. Replacement can be up to $13,000. Check for unusual noises or exhaust smells. |
| F1 Transmission Actuator Problems | Hard shifting or inability to shift gears. Can be very costly to replace the entire F1 kit ($20,000+) or around $3,400 for rebuilds. Test the F1 system thoroughly. |
| F1 Clutch Wear | Wears out quickly with aggressive driving. Costly to replace. Inquire about clutch life and driving style. |
| Sticky Interior Controls | Plastic components become sticky and deteriorate. Refinishing all interior pieces can cost $7,542 to $10,000. Inspect all buttons and surfaces. |
| Suspension Wear | Bushings and ball joints wear, causing handling issues or unusual sounds. Listen for clunks or squeaks over bumps. |
| Engine Mount Failures | Worn mounts lead to vibrations and a rougher driving experience. Check for excessive engine movement or vibrations. |
| Electrical Gremlins | Problems with power windows, lights, and ECU. Test all electrical components. |
| Carbon-Ceramic Rotors | High replacement costs if rotors wear out. Inspect rotor condition if equipped. |
