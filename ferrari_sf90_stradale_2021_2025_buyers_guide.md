# Ferrari SF90 Stradale 2021-2025 Buyer Guide

## 1. Introduction
The Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents a significant leap into the hybrid hypercar segment, offering unparalleled performance and technological sophistication. As a plug-in hybrid, it presents a unique value proposition for the discerning enthusiast, making it a compelling candidate for the Exotic Car Hacks philosophy. The primary risk associated with this model lies in the complexity of its advanced hybrid powertrain and sophisticated electronics, which can lead to unpredictable maintenance costs.

## 6. Known Problems and Recalls

**Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) is highly recommended for any used vehicle.**

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Infotainment System | Intermittent issues with Bluetooth connectivity and infotainment displays. Check all functions thoroughly during inspection. |
| Electrical System | General electrical problems, including 12V battery drain affecting car unlocking. Ensure all electrical components function correctly and check for battery health. |
| Turbocharger Oil Delivery Pipe | Potential for oil leaks and fire risk due to incorrectly manufactured pipe. Inspect for any signs of oil leakage around the turbochargers. |

### NHTSA Recalls

| NHTSA Recall | Campaign | Affected Years | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 23V-136 | Airbags could impact child seats | 2021-2023 (Stradale), 2022 (Spider) | Airbags could impact child seats. |
