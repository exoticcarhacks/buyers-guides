# Mercedes C63 AMG 2008-2015 Buyer Guide

The Mercedes C63 AMG 2008-2015 is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a compelling blend of raw power and everyday usability. It stands out as a strong hack candidate due to its significant depreciation from new, offering incredible performance value on the used market. The main risk associated with this model is the potential for costly engine-related issues, particularly in earlier model years, which can significantly impact ownership costs if not properly addressed.

## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) & Known Issues

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified independent Mercedes-Benz specialist is non-negotiable.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Head Bolt Failure | Early 2008–2011 models experienced head bolt corrosion, leading to coolant and oil mixing and significant engine damage. Mercedes addressed this by updating the head bolts in engines produced after 2011. Cost: Significant engine repair. |
| Camshaft and Lifter Wear | Premature wear of camshafts and lifters, causing ticking noises and potential engine performance issues, attributed to improper heat treatment of camshaft materials. Cost: High, engine internal repair. |
| Carbon Build-Up | M156 V8 engine is prone to carbon deposits on intake valves, leading to rough idling, reduced performance, and misfires. Cost: Regular carbon cleaning required. |
| Transmission Issues | Rough shifting or jerky movements due to faulty valve bodies or transmission control modules. Cost: Reprogramming or replacing components. |
| Suspension Wear | Performance-oriented suspension can experience wear, leading to clunking noises or a harsh ride. Worn bushings, control arms, or shock absorbers are common culprits. Cost: Replacement of worn components. |
| Electrical Issues | Malfunctioning sensors and infotainment system glitches. Cost: Diagnosis and repair by specialized expertise. |
| Brake Wear | Accelerated brake pad and rotor wear due to vehicle's weight and performance. Cost: Frequent replacement of pads and rotors. |
