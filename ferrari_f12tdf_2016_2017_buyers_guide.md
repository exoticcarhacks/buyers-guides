# Ferrari F12tdf 2016-2017 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Ferrari F12tdf 2016-2017 is a highly exclusive and potent machine, representing the pinnacle of Ferrari's front-engine V12 lineage. Its limited production run and track-focused enhancements make it a prime candidate for the discerning collector seeking both exhilarating performance and potential appreciation. The main risk lies in the specialized maintenance requirements and the potential for high repair costs if not meticulously inspected.
