# BMW X6 M Gen 1 2015-2019 Buyer Guide

The BMW X6 M Gen 1 (2015-2019) is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines aggressive styling with a potent 4.4L twin-turbo V8, making it a compelling hack candidate for those seeking sports car performance in a practical package. Its main risk lies in the potential for expensive engine and drivetrain repairs, particularly concerning oil leaks and transfer case issues.
