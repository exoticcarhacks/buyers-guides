## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified Mercedes-Benz specialist is non-negotiable for a vehicle of this complexity and performance.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Clicking sound from steering/suspension/axle/hub | A loud "clicking" sound at low speeds when turning, potentially caused by a bent rear wheel or issues with the hub/axle. Inspect wheels, tires, and suspension components for damage or excessive wear. |

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|

| VISIBILITY/WIPER & EXTERIOR LIGHTING | Rear lamps fogged up/not leaktight. Cause: Natural phenomenon under certain climatic conditions. Remedy: Clearing. Inspect for excessive or persistent fogging that does not clear, which could indicate a seal issue. |
| STRUCTURE | BODY & ELECTRICAL SYSTEM: Rear spoiler sporadically remains open or does not extend, with a fault message in the instrument cluster. Remedy: Re-establish function. Test the rear spoiler deployment and retraction multiple times. Check for any error messages in the instrument cluster. |
| POWER TRAIN | DSI selection lever module cannot be switched to park position "P" or "P" button malfunctions. Cause: Contamination/ingress of dust. Remedy: Not specified. Test the gear selector for smooth operation and ensure the "P" button engages correctly without hesitation. |
| VISIBILITY/WIPER & STRUCTURE | BODY: Washer fluid system leaks. Cause: Various possible causes including reservoir, seals, pump, or hose. Remedy: Not specified. Inspect the washer fluid reservoir, lines, and pump for any signs of leaks or residue. |
| ELECTRICAL SYSTEM | "Active Emergency Stop Assist Currently Unavailable" and "Active Steering Assist Inoperative" messages. Cause: Systems temporarily unavailable due to low tire pressure. Check tire pressures and ensure all driver assistance systems are functional and free of warning messages. |
