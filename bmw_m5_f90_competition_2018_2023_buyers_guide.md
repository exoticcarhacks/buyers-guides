# BMW M5 F90 Competition 2018-2023 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The BMW M5 F90 Competition (2018-2023) represents the pinnacle of BMW's performance sedan lineage, blending supercar-rivalling power with executive luxury. It stands as a compelling hack candidate due to its significant depreciation from new, offering immense performance value in the used market. The primary risk lies in the potential for complex electronic and mechanical issues inherent in high-performance, technologically advanced German vehicles.

A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a BMW specialist is non-negotiable for any M5 F90 Competition.
