# Ferrari Portofino and Portofino M 2018-2023 Buyer Guide

The Ferrari Portofino, introduced in 2018 as the successor to the California T, and its more potent evolution, the Portofino M, represent Ferrari's accessible grand touring convertible. Powered by a potent 3.9L twin-turbo V8, these models offer a compelling blend of performance and open-air driving, making them intriguing candidates for the discerning exotic car enthusiast seeking a "hackable" Ferrari. Potential buyers should be aware of the significant brake defect affecting these vehicles, which poses a critical safety risk and is a primary concern for ownership.

## Known Problems and Recalls


### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Power Top/Roof Mechanism Issues | Owners have reported persistent problems with the roof cycle, receiving error messages like “Complete roof cycle” and “Roof Failure.” Replacement roof can cost $26,000 before labor or tax. Hydraulic cylinder leak repair at a dealership can be $1,200 for labor plus $600 for parts. |
| Engine Cylinder Head Oil Leaks | Some owners have reported oil leaks from the cylinder heads, particularly around the valve cover gaskets. This can manifest as visible oil seepage or a burning oil smell. Inspect the cylinder heads and valve covers for any signs of leakage during a PPI. Repair costs can range from $3,000 to $4,500. |
| Gearbox Issues | Owners have reported various issues with the 7-speed (Portofino) and 8-speed (Portofino M) dual-clutch transmissions. Common failure modes include mechatronic unit malfunctions, sensor failures leading to incorrect gear selection or limp mode, and premature clutch pack wear, especially in cars subjected to aggressive driving or track use. Symptoms can include harsh shifts, delayed engagement, or warning lights. A complete DCT replacement can be extremely costly, with estimates around $45,000 (CAD), and repairs around $30,000 (CAD). Refurbished gearboxes are available for approximately $19,000 plus shipping and core charge. A thorough PPI should include a diagnostic scan for transmission fault codes and a test drive to assess shift quality. |
