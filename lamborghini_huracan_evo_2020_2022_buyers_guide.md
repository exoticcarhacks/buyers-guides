## Known Problems & Recalls
### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Misfires | Many owners have reported issues with engine misfires, which can be caused by a faulty spark plug, a clogged fuel injector, or a malfunctioning ignition system. This can lead to decreased performance, increased emissions, decreased fuel efficiency, potential engine damage, and triggering of the vehicle's check engine light. Reports have indicated that some engine misfire codes may appear and then disappear, leaving the owner unsure of the issue's origin. |
| Transmission | Some owners have noticed on their Huracan that they experience some clutch slippage, however, this is very rare. |
| Ticking noise | In early 2015, the fuel rail and injectors required replacement with an updated part due to a ticking noise. |
| No nose lift | Not having the optional nose lift causes an issue of a very scraped-up front bumper, especially since the Huracan is a very low car. So make sure to check underneath to see if there are any damages. |
| Computer problems/system warning messages | Reported in a 2020 Huracan Evo, with all lights and system warning messages coming on, possibly a computer issue. |
| Infotainment system issues | Discussed by owners. |
| Paint issues | Discussed by owners. |
| Steering squeal | Discussed by owners. |

### NHTSA Recalls
| NHTSA Recall | Campaign | Affected Years | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 22V-220 | N/A | 2020-2022 | The gearbox may have an insufficient amount of transmission oil, due to a supplier mistake, which can result in clutch slippage. Clutch slippage can cause a loss of drive power and increase the risk of a crash. Also, in certain driving situations the transmission oil can foam and leak out of the gear vent. Leaked transmission oil in the presence of hot exhaust system parts can increase the risk of a fire. |
| 22V081000 | N/A | 2015-2020 | The caps that cover the low-beam headlight horizontal adjustment screws are missing, which can allow the headlights to be improperly adjusted. |
| 21V466000 | N/A | 2018-2021 | In temperatures below 41F, the rearview camera may not display due to a software error in the infotainment system. |
