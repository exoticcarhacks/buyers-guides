# Ferrari Roma 2021-2024 Buyer Guide

## 1. Introduction
The Ferrari Roma, spanning model years 2021-2024, represents a compelling grand tourer that blends classic Ferrari elegance with modern performance. It stands out as a hack candidate due to its relatively accessible entry point into Ferrari ownership and its potential for value retention given its V8 powertrain and timeless design. The main risk lies in potential electrical and infotainment issues, which can be costly to address.

## 6. Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) & Known Issues
Always perform a comprehensive Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a Ferrari specialist.

### Known Problems
|Issue|What To Know/Check|
|---|---|
|Battery and Electrical Problems|Rapid discharge, electrical malfunctions, car failing to start after short periods. Cost: $200 - $700+ (for battery replacement, electrical diagnosis and repair can be significantly higher depending on the issue)|
|Engine Control System Failures|Warnings potentially linked to voltage sensitivities. Cost: $800 - $1,500+ (estimated for ECM replacement, actual costs can vary based on specific issue and diagnosis)|
|Interior and Infotainment Concerns|Touch interface lack of responsiveness and user-friendliness. Cost: Highly variable, from software updates (minimal) to full unit replacement ($1,500 - $3,500+ for advanced systems, potentially higher for Ferrari-specific units).|
|Steering Sensitivity|Described as overly sensitive or 'twitchy'. Cost: Highly variable, depends on diagnosis and specific component replacement if needed (e.g., steering rack, sensors).|

### NHTSA Recalls
|NHTSA Recall|Campaign|Affected Years|Issue|
|---|---|---|---|
|22V-536|22V-536|2020-2022|Service brakes, hydraulic. The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, which may lead to a partial or total loss of braking capability. This could result in injury or death to vehicle occupants. Warning: A warning light will appear on the dashboard when brake fluid decreases by approximately 50%. Later models also display "Brake fluid level low, Go to dealer slowly" and an acoustic buzzer.|
