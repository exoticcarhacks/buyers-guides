# Lamborghini Murcielago LP640 2007-2009 Buyer Guide

The Lamborghini Murcielago LP640, produced from 2007 to 2009, represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V12 performance and dramatic styling. As a prime candidate for the Exotic Car Hacks philosophy, its relative depreciation compared to newer models makes it an accessible entry into the world of true supercar ownership. However, the primary risk lies in the potential for significant maintenance and repair costs, particularly for engine-out services.

## Conclusion
The Lamborghini Murcielago LP640 is a **strong hack candidate** for those seeking an iconic V12 supercar experience without the astronomical entry price of its successors. The main risk remains the high cost of unexpected repairs, particularly those requiring engine-out service. The best ownership logic involves meticulous pre-purchase inspection, budgeting for potential maintenance, and enjoying the visceral thrill of a truly analog supercar before values potentially appreciate.
