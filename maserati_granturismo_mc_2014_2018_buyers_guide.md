# Maserati GranTurismo MC 2014-2018 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Maserati GranTurismo MC, produced from 2014 to 2018, represents the pinnacle of Maserati's grand touring coupe and convertible lineup, offering a blend of Italian design, a sonorous Ferrari-derived 4.7L V8 engine, and track-inspired performance. As a hack candidate, its depreciation curve has made it accessible, but the main risk lies in the potential for significant maintenance costs and electrical gremlins typical of exotic Italian machinery.

## Known Problems and NHTSA Recalls

Always obtain a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) from a qualified Maserati specialist before purchase.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Electrical glitches | Can manifest in various forms; check all electronics, lights, and sensors. |
| Variator issues | Listen for unusual engine noises, especially at startup. More common in earlier models, but still reported. |
| Transmission issues | Check for smooth shifts, no hesitation or harshness. |
| Engine oil pressure issues | Monitor oil pressure gauge, check for leaks, and ensure regular oil changes. |
| Radio hard drive rebooting in hot weather | Test infotainment system thoroughly, especially in warm conditions. |
| Oil changes | Budget for ~$600 per oil change; ensure service records are up to date. |

### NHTSA Recalls

| NHTSA Recall | Campaign | Affected Years | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 19V-461 | Airbag ECU | 2015-2018 | Airbag ECU with a defective power supply capacitor may not properly manage the voltage ripple of the 5V supply which may cause an incorrect function of the airbag ECU that may inhibit an airbag deployment or command an inadvertent deployment. |
