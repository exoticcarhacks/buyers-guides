# Lamborghini Aventador S 2017-2021 Buyer Guide

## 1. Introduction
The Lamborghini Aventador S (2017-2021) represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V12 performance, a true supercar icon. As a hack candidate, its depreciation curve has softened, offering an opportunity to experience extreme performance without the initial steep loss. The main risk, however, lies in the potential for astronomically expensive repairs, particularly concerning its complex gearbox and clutch.

## 8. Conclusion
The Lamborghini Aventador S is a strong hack candidate for those seeking an unadulterated V12 supercar experience. The main risk remains the potential for high-cost mechanical failures, particularly with the gearbox. Best ownership logic involves meticulous pre-purchase inspection, a robust maintenance fund, and understanding that while depreciation has slowed, running costs are significant.
