# Mercedes GLS63 AMG 2021-2025 Buyer Guide

## Introduction

The Mercedes GLS63 AMG 2021-2025 is a formidable high-performance luxury three-row SUV, blending opulent comfort with blistering AMG power. Its 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine with EQ Boost delivers 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque between 2,500-4,500 rpm. It presents a compelling hack candidate due to its significant depreciation curve, characteristic of high-end luxury performance vehicles. However, the primary risk lies in the complexity of its advanced systems, particularly the air suspension and intricate electronics, which can lead to substantial repair costs if not thoroughly vetted.
