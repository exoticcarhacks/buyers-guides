# Lamborghini Huracan Performante 2018-2019 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Lamborghini Huracan Performante 2018-2019 is a track-focused variant of the already potent Huracan, offering an exhilarating driving experience through its advanced active aerodynamics (ALA) and increased power. It stands as a prime candidate for the 'Exotic Car Hack' due to its significant performance capabilities and potential for value retention in the used market. The main risk associated with this model, as with many high-performance exotics, lies in the potential for costly repairs related to its complex systems.
