## Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) and Known Issues
Always perform a thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a qualified Bentley specialist. Focus on the following areas:

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Air Suspension Components | The air suspension system is complex. Listen for knocking noises from the front end, and check if the vehicle sits lower than usual, which could indicate worn components or leaks. Inspect air springs, compressor, and associated lines for signs of wear or damage. |
| High-Level Brake Light | The high-level brake light, located above the rear window, can fail. Check for proper illumination. Replacement often requires the entire light unit. |
