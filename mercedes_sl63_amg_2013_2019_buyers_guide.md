## Introduction
The Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG from 2013-2019, part of the R231 generation, represents a compelling proposition for the discerning enthusiast seeking a high-performance luxury roadster at a significant discount from its original MSRP. Powered by a potent 5.5L biturbo V8 (M157 engine), this vehicle offers exhilarating performance and opulent comfort, making it a prime candidate for the 'Exotic Car Hack' philosophy. However, potential buyers must be acutely aware of the complex Active Body Control (ABC) system, which, while providing exceptional handling, can be a source of costly repairs.

## Known Problems & Recalls
PPI reminder: Always get a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) from a qualified Mercedes-Benz specialist.

### Known Problems
| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Active Body Control (ABC) System | Potential failures in components such as struts and pumps. Check for leaks, uneven ride height, or warning messages. Repairs can be very costly. |
| Camshaft Position Sensors and Magnetic Adjusters | Oil wicking into wiring harness, damaging engine electronics. Look for oil residue around sensors and check for engine misfires or rough idling. |
| Retractable Hardtop Mechanism | Malfunctioning roof mechanisms, faulty sensors, or problems with the hydraulic system. Operate the roof multiple times during inspection, listen for unusual noises, and check for smooth operation. |
| Electrical System Problems | Central locking system, malfunctioning power windows, COMAND infotainment system glitches. Test all electrical components, including infotainment, windows, and locks. |
| Air Suspension Problems | Uneven ride height due to air leaks, failed air springs, or malfunctioning compressor. Check for sagging at corners or slow response when adjusting ride height. |
| Transmission Issues | Gear shifters stick when downshifting or shifting out of reverse. Pay attention to transmission behavior during test drive, especially during shifts. |
| LED Lighting Failures | Failures in front and rear lights. Verify all exterior lights are functioning correctly. |
