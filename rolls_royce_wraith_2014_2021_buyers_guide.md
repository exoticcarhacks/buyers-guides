# Rolls-Royce Wraith 2014-2021 Buyer Guide

## Introduction
The Rolls-Royce Wraith, produced from 2014 to 2021, represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury grand touring with a sporting edge. The main risk lies in the potentially exorbitant maintenance and repair costs associated with such a bespoke and complex vehicle.

## Conclusion
The Rolls-Royce Wraith offers unparalleled luxury and presence. The main risk is the potential for very high repair costs, particularly for engine-related issues. 

## Car Hacking Shortcuts

* Options to look for: Starlight Headliner, Bespoke Audio, Driver Assistance Systems, Black Badge Edition (if performance is a priority)
* PPI focus: Engine oil leaks, coolant leaks, turbocharger health, carbon buildup, and functionality of all electronic systems (especially infotainment and cameras).
* Known recalls: 0 recalls for 2014-2021, but 2 complaints for 2016 and various manufacturer communications exist. NHTSA search may be limited for ultra-luxury brands.
