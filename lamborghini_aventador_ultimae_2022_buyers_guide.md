## Common Problems and Recalls

### Known Problems

Issue | What To Know/Check
--- | ---
Single-clutch transmission | slow and clunky, especially at lower speeds; earlier models (2012-2015) are particularly affected (cost: high, for repair/replacement)
Clutch failure | wears out over time (cost: high, for replacement)
Electrical system issues | Common battery-related problems due to limited usage, leading to voltage drops and error codes. Check battery health and charging system. (cost: moderate to high)
Suspension wear and tear/leaky shocks | common, especially for older models (cost: high, for repair/replacement)
Engine overheating | Can occur in hot weather or track use. Common causes include low coolant levels or radiator fan malfunctions. Inspect coolant levels, radiator, and fan operation. (cost: variable, potentially high)
Brake wear | carbon ceramic brakes can glaze with light city use (cost: high, for replacement/resurfacing)
Tire wear | tires wear fast (cost: high, for frequent replacement)
Active aero spoiler issues | Reports of the active wing going into an active fault state or calibration issues, sometimes requiring an ECU flash. Inspect for proper deployment and retraction, and check for error codes. (cost: variable)
