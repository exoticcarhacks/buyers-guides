# BMW X5 M Gen 1 2015-2018 Buyer Guide

The BMW X5 M Gen 1 2015-2018 (F85 generation) represents BMW's high-performance entry into the mid-size luxury SUV segment. It's a compelling hack candidate due to its potent 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8, sophisticated xDrive all-wheel-drive system, and luxurious appointments, offering supercar-level performance in a practical package at a significant depreciation from its original MSRP. The main risk lies in the potential for costly repairs associated with its complex powertrain and advanced electronics.
