# BMW X5 M Gen 1 2015-2018 Buyer Guide

The BMW X5 M Gen 1 2015-2018 (F85 generation) represents BMW's high-performance entry into the mid-size luxury SUV segment. It's a compelling hack candidate due to its potent 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8, sophisticated xDrive all-wheel-drive system, and luxurious appointments, offering supercar-level performance in a practical package at a significant depreciation from its original MSRP. The main risk lies in the potential for costly repairs associated with its complex powertrain and advanced electronics.

## Known Problems & Recalls

A thorough Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) by a BMW specialist is non-negotiable.

### Known Problems

| Issue | What To Know/Check |
|---|---|
| Oil Leaks | particularly from the valve cover gaskets and oil filter housing gaskets. Can cause oil to drip onto other engine components. Cost: Not specified, but can lead to more severe issues. |
| Water Leakage | Sunroof drain tubes can become clogged or damaged, leading to water leaks into the cabin. Can result in wet carpets and damage to electrical components. Cost: Not specified. |
| Engine Overheating | Often linked to a malfunctioning water pump. Can lead to expensive repairs. Cost: Not specified. |
| Transmission Issues | ZF 8-speed automatic transmission can develop problems like harsh shifting, slipping, and complete transmission failure. Cost: Not specified. |
| Engine Misfires | Often caused by failing spark plugs, ignition coils, or injectors. Leads to loss of power and poor fuel efficiency. Cost: Not specified. |
| Noisy Doors | Wind noise from the doors at higher speeds due to misaligned door seals or faulty window trim. Cost: Not specified. |
| Creases on seats | particularly on the driver's side bolster. Cost: Not specified. |
| Squeak noise when steering | often due to a faulty clock spring. Cost: Not specified (easy fix mentioned). |
| Rattles from front suspension | can be due to front stabilizer or steering rack. Cost: Not specified. |
| Rattle from center console | can be fixed by greasing with silicone spray. Cost: Not specified. |
| Rattle from dashboard | specifically from the chrome end cap piece on the passenger side. Cost: Not specified. |
