## Conclusion
The Mercedes G63 AMG (W464) is a **strong hack candidate** due to its robust demand and often appreciating value, especially for well-optioned examples. The main risk lies in its **exorbitant running costs and potential for high-cost mechanical failures**. Best ownership logic involves meticulous pre-purchase inspections, understanding the total cost of ownership, and strategic option selection to maximize resale value.

## Car Hacking Shortcuts
* Years to buy: 2019-2025 (W464 generation)
* Options to look for: Night Package, Exclusive Interior Package Plus, AMG Trail Package (if off-road focused)
* Preferred colors: Black, White, G Manufaktur colors (e.g., Desert Sand, Classic Grey) tend to hold value well.
* PPI focus: Thorough inspection of suspension components, brakes, radiator, and electrical systems. Verify all recalls are addressed.
* Known recalls: Verify all NHTSA recalls by VIN, especially those related to braking, door latches, and fuel leaks.
* Market forecast: Stable to appreciating for well-maintained, desirable configurations. Monitor market trends closely.
